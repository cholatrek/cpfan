About Cpfan

Cocoa and Plantain Farmers
Association of Nigeria

CPFAN is an association of progressive and collective-minded Farmers that is poised to up-scale the local production of plantain and Cocoa as major export commodities in Nigeria.

The association was launched in the year 2020 and so far has built a membership base in 26 states across the six geopolitical zones of the country. The membership presently has grown to over 76,000 and more farmers are still joining the association.

Our vision

To meet the demand for cocoa and plantain-based raw materials from both domestic and international industries
